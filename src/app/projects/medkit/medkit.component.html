Description

Medkit was my graduation project and its main features are :

• Healthcare Community : Postting health related problems and questions and get responses form doctors from around the world

• Online Clinic : Book an appointment with doctors and chat with them, using our chat service and live video call then get your prescription after it

• Emergency Call : In case you are in danger or you need help You can send an emergency alert to up to 3 people you have selected and trust, with your current location
